About FitTrybe

THIS IS YOUR
STAT SHEET.
OWN IT.

Built by Darrien Schleifer

Most people go to the gym, put in the work, and leave with nothing to show for it — no record of what they lifted, no idea how far they've come, no target to chase next session.

FitTrybe was built to fix that. Not as some massive all-in-one platform. Not loaded with features nobody asked for. Just a clean, focused stat sheet that does exactly what a serious lifter needs — log your lifts, track your bests, set real goals, and see your progress laid out in front of you.

Because numbers don't lie. When you can see that your squat is up 40 lbs from where it started, or that you've hit the gym 148 times this year, or that you're 91% of the way to a goal you set six months ago — that's fuel. That's the thing that gets you back in the next day.

Simple by design

No bloat. No noise. Just the numbers that matter.

Progress is the point

Every session logged is proof you showed up.

Goals keep you honest

Set the target. Chase it. Hit it. Set a new one.
